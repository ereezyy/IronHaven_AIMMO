## Full VO script

**[0–12]**  
This is IronHaven — a free cyberpunk multiplayer game that runs in your browser. No install. No launcher. Open the link and you’re on the street.

**[12–28]**  
Create a callsign. Choose your archetype — Runner, Enforcer, Ghost, or Fixer. Kit modular gear. Your look syncs to other players.

**[28–50]**  
The city is a systems sandbox: combat, vehicles, police heat, jobs, shops, factions. Neon Syndicate, Chrome Guard, Dock Rats — pick a color or stay unaffiliated.

**[50–65]**  
Other players share the same metropolis in real time. Presence, chat, leaderboards, world events when districts heat up.

**[65–80]**  
IronHaven is early live and growing. Core play is free. Optional IronHaven Pass is $1.99 a week if you want to fund the next blocks.

**[80–90]**  
Zero install. Full neon. Your streets.  
Play free at ironhaven-aimmo.vercel.app.
