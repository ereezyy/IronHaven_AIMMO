## One-liner

A free browser cyberpunk MMO: build your operator, claim the streets, play with others — zero install.

---

## Elevator (50 words)

IronHaven drops you into a neon metropolis that runs entirely in the browser. Create a street operator, customize modular gear, fight, drive, and take jobs while other players share the same city in real time. Optional weekly Pass. No launcher. No download. Just jack in.

---

## Key features (bullet-ready)

- **Instant play** — open the URL, enter the city
- **Character creator** — 4 archetypes + modular avatar parts
- **Live multiplayer** — see players, sync appearance, chat
- **Living systems** — combat, vehicles, police, economy, factions
- **District pressure** — territory, world events, leaderboards
- **Cyberpunk atmosphere** — night city lighting, weather, VFX
- **IronHaven Pass** — optional $1.99/week support tier

- **Commerce:** Stripe Payment Link + webhooks
- **Node target:** 22.x (build/tooling)
