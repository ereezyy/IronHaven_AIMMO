## IronHaven AIMMO Opens Its Neon Streets — Free Browser Cyberpunk MMO Goes Live

**Instant-play multiplayer city built with React, Three.js, and Supabase; optional $1.99 weekly Pass**

---

**[City / Online] — [Date]** — Independent developer **ereezyy** today opened **IronHaven AIMMO**, a free-to-play cyberpunk multiplayer game that runs entirely in the browser. Players can create a street operator, customize modular gear, and drop into a shared neon city with no download and no launcher.

IronHaven combines real-time multiplayer presence, district-scale systems (combat, vehicles, economy, factions, world events), and a modern web stack: **React**, **TypeScript**, **Three.js / React Three Fiber**, **Rapier** physics, **Supabase** realtime backend, and **Vercel** hosting.

> “Most ‘web games’ are toys or single-player demos. IronHaven is built as a city you share with other people the moment you open a tab,” said ereezyy, creator of IronHaven. “Zero install is the feature. The streets are the product.”

### What players get at launch

- **Character creator** with four archetypes — Runner, Enforcer, Ghost, Fixer — and modular avatar parts that sync to other players
- **Live multiplayer** position and appearance sync, chat, and online presence
- **Open-city systems** including combat, vehicles, police heat, shops, missions, harvesting, and more
- **Factions** — Neon Syndicate, Chrome Guard, Dock Rats — plus territory-linked world events and leaderboards
- **IronHaven Pass** ($1.99/week via Stripe) for players who want to support ongoing development

Core gameplay remains free. The Pass is optional.

### About IronHaven

IronHaven is an independent browser MMO set in a neon-soaked metropolis where street operators carve out reputation, money, and turf. The project ships as a living production build with an open development posture on GitHub.
