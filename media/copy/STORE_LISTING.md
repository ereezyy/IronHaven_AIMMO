## Long description

```
IRONHAVEN AIMMO

Zero install. Full neon. Your streets.

IronHaven is a free-to-play cyberpunk multiplayer game that runs entirely in your browser. Create a street operator, customize modular gear, and drop into a shared metropolis with real players.

FEATURES
• Instant play — open the link, enter the city
• Character creator — Runner, Enforcer, Ghost, Fixer
• Modular avatar parts synced across multiplayer
• Combat, vehicles, police heat, and street economy
• Factions — Neon Syndicate, Chrome Guard, Dock Rats
• Missions, shops, harvest, hunt, fish, black market
• Live multiplayer presence, chat, leaderboards
• Dynamic world events on district pressure
• Optional IronHaven Pass ($1.99/week)

CONTROLS
WASD move · Mouse look · Shift sprint · Space jump
E interact · LMB attack · Tab inventory

REQUIREMENTS
Modern desktop browser with WebGL 2.0 (Chrome / Edge / Firefox recommended).

LINKS
Play: https://ironhaven-aimmo.vercel.app
Source: https://github.com/ereezyy/IronHaven_AIMMO
```
