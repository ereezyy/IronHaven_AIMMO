# Public FAQ (paste into Discord / site / PH)

## What is IronHaven?

A free cyberpunk multiplayer game that runs in your browser. Build a street operator and share a neon city with other players — no download.

## Where do I play?

https://ironhaven-aimmo.vercel.app

## Is it free?

Yes. Core gameplay is free. **IronHaven Pass** is an optional $1.99/week support subscription.

## What do I need?

A desktop browser with WebGL 2.0 (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox recommended). A mouse and keyboard.

## Does it work on mobile?

Mobile is not the primary target for v1.0. Some phones may load, but expect desktop-first controls and performance.

## Is this crypto / Web3 / NFT?

No. No wallet required. Payments (Pass) use normal Stripe checkout.

## How does multiplayer work?

Players in the shared city sync through Supabase Realtime (presence, position, appearance, chat).

## What can I do in the city?

Create a character, fight, drive, take jobs, trade in shops, join faction fantasy, hit world events, climb leaderboards — systems are live and expanding.

## Who made this?

Independent developer **ereezyy**. Source: https://github.com/ereezyy/IronHaven_AIMMO

## How do I report a bug?

GitHub Issues: https://github.com/ereezyy/IronHaven_AIMMO/issues  
Include browser, GPU if known, and steps to reproduce.

## Can I use screenshots / clips?

Yes for non-commercial press and social sharing of the game. Credit **IronHaven AIMMO** and link the play URL when practical. Third-party art packs keep their original licenses.

## Roadmap?

Post-1.0 themes: richer avatar catalog, missions by district, guild/party tooling, performance, expanded Pass rewards. Community feedback prioritizes order.
